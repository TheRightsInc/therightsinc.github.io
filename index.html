Rolling out the red carpet
(Without the red tape)

Traditionally music licensing has been a messy, time-consuming, endeavor. The Rights is simplifying the process by creating a modern marketplace that enables catalog managers and content creators to easily collaborate.

As the music industry struggles to address this influx of potential new licensees with a manual licensing process, The Rights are automating the most time-consuming parts, allowing all parties to capitalize on the wealth of opportunities.

For Rights Holders

A Catalog Management tool that's tailored to your needs.

Regain time

We minimize transaction costs by taking the busy work out of the most simple transactions, leaving only the smallest amount of work to do manually.

Jump on opportunities

Easily capitalize on missed opportunities from long-tail content, like social media, videos and podcasts. Know exactly who’s using your content and how to price offers accordingly.

Keep track of everything

Our centralized system makes it easy to manage approvals, renewals and compliance.

For Content Creators
